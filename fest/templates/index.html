What's it all about

A new acro festival has born in the swiss mountains!
From 8 till 11 of March we'd like to train, play and have fun with all acrobats, athletes, performers and teachers in this four days. Since we're in the mountains we'll add some Snow related activities. And to relax - after hard training days - there will be a evening to enjoy some Warm out-door Whirlpool at a local hotel.

General information

  • Accommodation

    We have a cosy holiday house with dormitories for 50 People. *Not included in daily tickets. More important details CLICK HERE

  • Food

    Main dishes, healthy snacks and fruits are all vegan and included. We have a main Cook but there will be some help asked of everyone.

  • Workshops

    There will be several rounds of workshops and a constant Jam. Experience in acro is necessary, there will be no beginner workshops.WORKSHOP PLAN HERE

  • Special events

    On friday noon ther's an out-door training up on the mountain in the ski resort. (If the weather is good ). There will be a warm out-door pool to relax to choose between thursday, friday or saturday evening.

  • Location

    The whole event takes place in Lenk im Simmental Switzerland. A complete description on how to get there can be found HERE

  • Prices

    We have passes based on the amount of days you stay at the festival. All of them include: accomodation, food, workshops, local train and lift tickets, etc...
    1 day tickets don't include breakfast. More information, like list of prices and conditions CLICK HERE
