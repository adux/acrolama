What's it all about

A New Acro Convetion has born in the Swiss Mountains!
From 8 till 11 of March we'd like to train, play and have fun with all acrobats, therapists, athletes and teachers in this four intence days. Since we're in the mountais we'll add some Snow related activities. To relax after a hard training day there will be a evening to enjoy some Warm outside Whirlpool.

General Information

  • Accomodation

    We have a cosy holiday house and there are dormitories for 50 People. * Not included in daily tickets

  • Food

    Main dishes, healthy snacks and fruits are all Vegan and included. We have a main Cook but there will be some help asked of everyone.

  • Workshops

    There will be several rounds of Workshops and a constant Jam. Experience in Acro is necessary, there will be no begginer Workshops but alot of friendly people. Full Schedule soon.

  • Special Events

    On friday noon ther's a out-door training up on the mountain in the ski resort. (If the weather is good ). The transportation is provided. There will be a Warm out-door Pool to relax Friday and Saturday evening.

  • Location

    The hole event Takes place in Lenk im simmental Switzerland. A complete description on how to get there can be found HERE

  • Prices

    We have Passes based on the amout of days you stay at the festival. All of them include: Accomodation, Food, Workshops, Special Events, etc...
    Daily Tickets on the other hand don't include breakfast. For more infor like list of prices and conditions please go HERE
