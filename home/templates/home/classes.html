Classes

CYCLE

In order to balance progress and compromise our Classes are wrapped into Cycles. Each cycle has a topic to deepen into for 4 weeks. Drop ins are possible and a good oportunity to test which level and teacher fits you best.

Level:

We want you to feel comfortable but with the right amount of challenge. Cycles are split in 4 levels. You can always swap levels as long as you meet the prerequisites.

Partner:

In General only advanced acrobatic classes require you to come with a partner. We keep classes balanced on each roll so you always have someone to train with.
