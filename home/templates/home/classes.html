Cycles:

Each cycle has its own topic and consists of 4 weekly classes. This format, balances progress and compromise, helping you get the most out of it.

Level:

We want you to feel comfortable but with the right amount of challenge. Cycles are split in 4 levels you can always swap as long as you meet the prerequisites.

Only advanced classes require you to come with a partner. We keep classes balanced on each roll.
